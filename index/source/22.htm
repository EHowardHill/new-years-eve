I was dreaming of my previous life again.

I was at my small, cottage home, grabbing some food before I went back out into the fields. There was a knock at the door. This wasn't uncommon.

It was Wanu. "Moon, we need to talk."

"Hm? What about? It is Roro?" Roro was his fiance.

"Sort of." he sighed. "I'm asking her out to the festival tonight. It's about Uo. I want tonight to be about Roro and I, and I'm afraid he won't want to leave us alone. I was wondering if you could.. uh.. keep him busy tonight."

Laughing a bit under my breath, I felt relieved. "I'll do my best." I supposed the fields could wait for an evening. After all, the festival wasn't going to be in the village all season.

"Thank you, Moon. I'll buy you a tray of cookies or something."
